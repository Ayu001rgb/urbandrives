Terms & Conditions

Please read the rental policies carefully before booking.

Last Updated: October 2025

1 Eligibility & Documents

The driver must be at least 21 years old.

A valid Original Driving License is mandatory. Learners license is NOT accepted.

Aadhar Card / Voter ID verification is required at the time of pickup.

2 Security Deposit

A refundable security deposit of ₹3,000 to ₹5,000 (depending on the car model) is required. This amount will be refunded within 24 hours after the trip ends, provided there are no damages or challans.

3 Fuel & Tolls

Fuel: The car is provided with a certain fuel level and must be returned with the same level. If returned with less fuel, charges will apply.

Fastag/Tolls: All tolls and parking charges are to be paid by the user. Fastag usage will be deducted from the security deposit.

4 Speed Limit & Damages

The speed limit is 80 km/h - 100 km/h (as per government norms). Over-speeding penalty is ₹500 for the first instance.

In case of an accident or damage, the user is liable to pay for repairs up to a maximum cap of ₹10,000. Insurance covers the rest.

5 Cancellation Policy

  • 0% Deduction: If cancelled before 24 hours of pickup time.
  • 50% Deduction: If cancelled within 24 hours of pickup time.
  • No Refund: In case of No-Show or cancellation after pickup time.
