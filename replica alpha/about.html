Our Story

Driving Mumbai Since 2015

Urban Drive started with a simple vision: to give Mumbaikars the freedom to drive without the hassle of owning a car. What began with just 5 cars in a small garage in Andheri has now grown into a fleet of 100+ premium vehicles.

We understand the fast-paced life of Mumbai. Whether it's a corporate meeting in BKC or a chilling weekend in Lonavala, our cars are maintained to give you zero trouble.
