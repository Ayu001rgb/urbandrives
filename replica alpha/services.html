Service FAQs

Is fuel included in the price?

No, we follow a "Tank-to-Tank" policy. You will receive the car with a specific fuel level, and you must return it with the same level.

Is FASTag available?

Yes, all our cars are equipped with active FASTags. The toll amount incurred during your trip will be deducted from your security deposit upon return.

What happens if the car breaks down?

We provide 24/7 Roadside Assistance (RSA). In case of a mechanical breakdown, simply call our helpline, and we will arrange support or a replacement vehicle.
